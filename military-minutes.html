Military Minutes


Check out Dr. Carrie Elk’s new NBC WLFA recurring segment “Military Minutes.” You can watch locally 8:30-9:00 every other Tuesday (beginning 3/6/18) or watch worldwide on WLFA’s Facebook live page!

Dr. Elk comments; “This is an exciting opportunity to share what I have learned from the military community about supporting them with the larger audience to create a greater appreciation and sense of community for those who serve!”
